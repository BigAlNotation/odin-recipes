Lasagna Recipe

This is how to make Lasagna

Homemade Lasagna is a classic that every cook should have in there book. Tender sheets of pasta, a cheese filling, and a rich meaty tomato sauce make the perfect dish!

Ingredients needed as follows:

  • Cheese Filling
  • Meat Sauce
  • Layers

  1. First make the meat sauce
  2. Then boil the pasta
  3. Then layer thr sheets
  4. Last add cheese and filling as you layer
